Egg Salad

My Egg Salad

This egg salad is delicious and delectable. There is no better egg salad on the market. Good luck trying to find a better one!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayo
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Dill

Steps

  1. Hard boil the eggs and smash them in a bowl
  2. Add 1 tbsp of mayo per egg
  3. Add 1/2 tsp of apple cider vinegar per egg
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add one sprig of fresh dill per one egg
  6. Chill in refrigerator for at least one hour
