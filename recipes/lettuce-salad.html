Lettuce Salad

My Lettuce Salad

This lettuce salad is delicious and delectable. There is no better lettuce salad on the market. Good luck trying to find a better one!

Ingredients

  • Spring Mix Lettuce
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Mushrooms
  • Ranch dressing

Steps

  1. Lay out lettuce on a plate or bowl
  2. Chop grape tomatoes in half
  3. Slice cucumbers
  4. Roughly chop mushrooms
  5. Top the lettuce with all of the vegetables
  6. Add ranch dressing on top of the salad
